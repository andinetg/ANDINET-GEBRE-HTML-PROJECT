OUR FOOD MENU

  1. Misir Wot: Lentils cooked with onions and garlic in spiced red pepper sauce to satisfy non meat eaters

  2. Kik Alicha: Split peas cooked in onion, garlic and turmeric flavored sauce.

  3. Gomen (Collard Greens):Steamed and delicately seasoned with garlic and green chilies mixed with potatoes and carrots.

  4. Tikel Gomen:Cabbage cooked with carrots in onion and garlic sauce and flavored in Ethiopian spices.

  5. Shiro Wot:A popular Ethiopian dish with chickpeas and split peas powder made into a delicious saucemixed with onion, garlic and jalapeño pepper.

  6. Fosolia:String beans, carrots, onion and garlic all sautéed in specially blended Ethiopian spices.

  7. Denich Wot :Potato stew made in medium spicy curry and cumin sauce.

  8. Difin Misir (Azifa):Organic whole lentil salad with onions, jalapeño, olive oil, lemon juice and herbs.

  9. Key Ser :Cooked beets and potatoes seasoned with carrots,onions, jalapeño peppers sautéed in vegetable oil.

  10. Veggie Combo :A combination of six popular vegetarian dishes.

  11. Bozena Shiro:A popular Ethiopian dish with beef. Shiro is chickpeas and split peas powder made into a delicious sauce cooked with onion, garlic and spiced Ethiopian butter.

  12. Doro Wot:Tender chicken marinated in lemon sautéed in seasoned Ethiopian butter and stewed in light pepper sauce, potatoes, carrots, garlic and ginger root served with hard-boiled eggs.

  13. Quanta Firfir:Specially prepared dried meat (traditional beef jerky) cooked in a spicy sauce and mixed with injera:Boneless, skinless cubed chicken sautéed in a special blend of spices and braised in onions, tomato, green pepper and purified butter. Served hot or mild.

  14. Special Ye’Beg Tibs (Lamb):Special Ye’Beg Tibs (Lamb): Cubes of tenderloin lamb sautéed with Ethiopian butter, onion, jalapeños and our Chef’s Special Sauce.

  15. Alicha Fitfit (Lamb):Fine lamb meat and bone cooked in ginger, garlic, and onion tossed with injera.

  16. Dulet (Lamb):Lamb trip and liver mixed with lean red meat, diced onions, garlic, jalapeño, Ethiopian butter, cardamom and mitmita.
